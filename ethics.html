MUSIC

  • Gultrah Sound System

    • Gultrah Sound System stands out as a revolutionary voice in the Tunisian music scene, blending the rhythms of reggae, afrobeat, funk, and jazz with profound social and political commentary. Founded in 2006 by Halim Yousfi, Wissem Ziadi, and Malek Halim, the band is known for its commitment to highlighting the struggles and hopes of the Tunisian people. Their lyrics are a fearless reflection of life under repression, infused with humor, defiance, and cultural pride. Yousfi’s powerful and emotive voice leads the band’s sound, which pairs energetic beats with violin melodies and percussive vigor, creating a magnetic and soul-stirring atmosphere. Whether addressing themes of freedom, inequality, or resilience, Gultrah Sound System offers not only unforgettable music but also a beacon of hope and resistance for the youth of Tunisia and beyond, resonating deeply with audiences who dream of change and justice. Gultrah Sound System

  • Mickael Jackson

    • Michael Jackson was a revolutionary artist whose influence changed the landscape of music forever. Known as the King of Pop, he broke boundaries with iconic albums like Thriller, which remains the best-selling album in history. His music videos, such as “Thriller” and “Billie Jean,” elevated the art form, blending cinematic storytelling with unforgettable choreography. SPORT

  • Club Sportif Sfaxien

    • Club Sportif Sfaxien (CSS), established in 1928 and based in the vibrant city of Sfax, is one of Tunisia’s most distinguished football clubs. Known for their iconic black-and-white colors, CSS has earned widespread acclaim both domestically and on the continental stage. They have claimed multiple Tunisian Ligue 1 titles, illustrating their dominance in national competitions, and have achieved notable success in the CAF Confederation Cup, winning it four times, which is the highest in the tournament's history. Their home, the Stade Taïeb Mhiri, seats 18,000 passionate supporters who have witnessed countless historic matches over the decades. Beyond football, Club Sportif Sfaxien also competes successfully in volleyball and basketball, showcasing the club’s dedication to athletic excellence across sports. The legacy of CSS is deeply interwoven with the spirit and culture of Sfax, making it a pillar of pride for the local community and an influential name in African and Arab football.Club Sportif Sfaxien (CSS)

  • Cristiano Ronaldo

    • Cristiano Ronaldo is widely regarded as one of the greatest footballers of all time, known for his extraordinary skills and unparalleled work ethic. With an impressive career that includes stints at Manchester United, Real Madrid, and Juventus, he has set numerous records, including being the all-time top scorer in the UEFA Champions League. Ronaldo's remarkable athleticism and technical ability have made him a prolific goal-scorer and a key player in every team he has joined. Beyond his achievements on the field, he is admired for his leadership and determination, often inspiring his teammates to strive for excellence. Off the pitch, Ronaldo is also recognized for his philanthropic efforts, contributing significantly to various charitable causes, including children's hospitals. With multiple Ballon d'Or awards to his name, his legacy as a football icon is firmly established, serving as a role model for aspiring athletes worldwide. His journey from humble beginnings in Madeira to global superstardom exemplifies how hard work and dedication can lead to incredible success​. Cristiano Ronaldo

  • Real Madrid

    • Real Madrid, founded in 1902, is celebrated as one of the most successful football clubs in history, known for its iconic white kit and rich heritage. The club boasts a remarkable record of 34 La Liga titles and 14 UEFA Champions League trophies, making it a symbol of excellence in both Spanish and European football. The Santiago Bernabéu Stadium, named after the club's legendary president, serves as a historic venue where countless memorable matches have taken place, creating an electric atmosphere for fans. Real Madrid's success can be attributed to its ability to attract and develop world-class talent, featuring legendary players such as Alfredo Di Stéfano, Cristiano Ronaldo, and Karim Benzema. The club's commitment to a winning culture has fostered a legacy of greatness that inspires millions around the globe. As it continues to build on its storied past, Real Madrid remains a beacon of ambition and achievement in the world of sports, relentlessly pursuing new triumphs. Real Madrid

VIDEO GAMES

  • Pro Evolution Soccer 6 (PES 6)

    • Pro Evolution Soccer 6 (PES 6) holds a special place in the hearts of football video game enthusiasts. Released in 2006, this iconic title from Konami captured the essence of the beautiful game with its fluid gameplay, strategic depth, and realistic ball physics. Unlike many of its contemporaries, PES 6 delivered an immersive experience that balanced simplicity with skillful control, allowing both casual players and hardcore fans to enjoy thrilling matches. Memorable features like the Master League, which let players build their dream teams and compete in long-term campaigns, added a layer of depth that kept gamers coming back for more. The game also boasted a roster filled with legendary players and classic teams, supported by a soundtrack that brought energy to every match. Despite advances in gaming technology since its release, PES 6 remains a nostalgic masterpiece, fondly remembered for its charm and gameplay excellence.

  • Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas

    • Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas is an action-adventure video game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games in 2004. Set in the fictional state of San Andreas, inspired by California and Nevada, the game follows Carl "CJ" Johnson, a young man returning to his hometown to investigate his mother's murder, rebuild his gang, and uncover the truth behind a series of conspiracies. Combining open-world exploration, story-driven missions, and extensive player freedom, GTA: San Andreas is acclaimed for its rich narrative, diverse gameplay, and cultural impact.
